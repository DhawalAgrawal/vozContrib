Explore your passion with VOZ

VOZ is contributor fueled because we believe in our contributors’ passion for their interests and hobbies. VOZ acts as the voice of a community by providing the best stories, videos, updates and interactive content related to myriad topics as well as a space that sparks social conversations through its unique discussion feature. Conventional news has lost its charm and it’s time to revolutionize content and this is an opportunity for all of us to create a social media for the content-hungry generation.

What are we looking for?

  • An avid TV series, movies, sports or politics enthusiast.
  • Creative & passionate individuals with a flair for content writing.
  • Dedicated individuals who commit an hour daily to their community daily.

What’s in it for me?

  • Become an Influencer.
  • We expose your content and work to professionals in the creative industry.
  • Certificates by VOZ and E-cell, IITK and monetary benefits are also a possibility.
  • Improve through feedback from your community while you channel your passion and inner fandom.
  • Awards, merchandise, coupons, and other cool stuff to be earned.
  • Enjoy many more perks and benefits as we grow and thrive. Stay put and you’ll be a part of something great.

Journey to become a VOZ contributor

  • Signup with the application form.
  • Complete 3 fun writing quests.
  • Receive an invite to join after completion of the process.

Give us your passion & we will give you an audience. Join us on this mission to create the social media for the next generation.
