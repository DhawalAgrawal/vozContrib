Explore your passion at VOZ

VOZ is run by our contributor because of their passion for their interests. VOZ acts as a voice of a community by providing best stories, videos, updates & interactive content related to any curiosity of yours, as well as a space that sparks social conversations through a unique discussion feature. With users hailing from youth bored of conventional news & ideas it’s time to revolutionize content and opportunity for all of us to create a social media for the next of content-hungry generation.

What are we looking for?

  • An avid TV series, movies, sports or politics enthusiast
  • Creative & passionate individuals with a flair of content writing.
  • A commitment to managing the communities by contributing a minimum of 1 hr daily.

What’s in it for me?

  • Your passion for work.
  • Become an Influencer.
  • We expose your content and work to professionals in the creative industry.
  • Awards, merchandise, coupons, and other cool stuff to be earned.
  • Certificates issued from VOZ attested from E-cell, IITK and monetary benefits are also a possibility.
  • Enjoy many more perks and benefits as we grow and thrive. Stay put and you’ll be a part of something great.

Journey to become a VOZ contributor

  • Signup using the application form below
  • Complete 3 fun writing quests
  • Receive an invite to join after completion of the process.

Give us your passion & we will give you an audience. Join us on this mission to create the social media for the next generation.
