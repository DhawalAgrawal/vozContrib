VOZ Contributor Program FAQ

What are the criteria & requirement to become a VOZ contributor’?

Only quality we look in our contributors is their love & passion for the communities of their interest. Every individual of 18 and above are eligible to contribute to VOZ. As of now we only have a web version so an individual must possess a laptop or a PC, the mobile and tab version are under progress.

What type of content will contributors work on?

That’s really flexible, You can contribute any content piece that a fan like you would love to consume. But for the exact guidelines, we will provide you a manual after you are selected.

Will I be able to choose the content of my choice?

Yes, you can, we promote our contributors to use their creativity & passion in the aggregation of content. But the content pieces have to be in the guidelines mentioned in the contributor manual.

How many content pieces I have to contribute?

We at VOZ don’t believe in exact numbers. It’s your passion that makes you contribute, you can contribute as much as you can but we expect a daily minimum time of 1hr to be given by each contributor at VOZ.

Will the content piece be bylined by our real name?

We encourage VOZ Contributors to use their real name; however, we understand there are some situations where this is not desirable, and we can work with you to find an alternative solution.

Will contributors be paid for every content piece?

Not as of now, but we will provide you all the perks mentioned by us. In cases of exceptional performances, contributors will be provided with monetary incentives. We are working towards our mission of creating the social media for the next generation. But monetization is a possibility for sure in future, as we say stay put and you will be part of something big.
